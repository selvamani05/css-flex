President's Message
We are very excited to celebrate Walden Savings Bank’s 145th anniversary this year. How have we made it this long? That’s easy. It’s because of you, our loyal customers and community! We want to say "Thank You" by inviting all of you to join us as we celebrate several branch milestones this year, including Walden (145 years), Scott’s Corners (15 years) and Middlehope (10 years). As always, we will be holding free community shred days in each of these locations and the public is always welcome! In 2017, we will also continue our support for the many charitable organizations that impact our community. If you’re not currently a Walden Savings Bank customer, there’s no better time to give us a try. We are committed to providing our customers with the latest digital banking technology, excellent financial products, and superior customer service. We thank you for your continued support and look forward to exceeding your personal and business banking expectations in the years to come.
